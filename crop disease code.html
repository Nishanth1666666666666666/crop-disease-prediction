Tomato Late Blight
High Confidence

🔬 CAUSE

Caused by Phytophthora infestans. Spreads rapidly in cool (10–20°C), wet weather. Dark water-soaked spots with white mould on leaf undersides. Can destroy entire crop within days.

✅ WHAT TO DO NOW — STEP BY STEP

Step 1: URGENT — Remove and BURN all infected plant parts immediately. Do not compost. Step 2: Spray Metalaxyl + Mancozeb (Ridomil Gold MZ) at 2.5g per litre on all plants urgently. Step 3: Stop overhead irrigation immediately — switch to drip irrigation only.

💊 TREATMENT

Apply Metalaxyl 8% + Mancozeb 64% WP (Ridomil Gold) 2.5g/L every 5–7 days. Alternate with Cymoxanil + Mancozeb (Curzate M8) for resistance management.

🛡️ PREVENTION

Use certified disease-free seeds. Plant resistant varieties (Arka Rakshak). Ensure field has good drainage. Never irrigate in the evening.
